A Note from the Wine Director

The wine list of Maison Vie is conceived as a quiet dialogue between two cultures — the classic voices of France and the generous accents of the New World — spoken in harmony with the terroirs and aromatic herbs of Vietnam. Every bottle listed here has been selected to accompany a specific gesture of the chef: the delicate maturation of Khánh Hòa scallops, the slow render of Vietnamese duck breast, the umami precision of miso-marinated cod, the smoky authority of Wagyu.

List rượu của Maison Vie được kiến tạo như một cuộc đối thoại tĩnh lăng giữa hai nền văn hoá — tiếng nói cổ diễn của Pháp và giong điều hào phóng của Tân Thế Giới — hoà nhịp cùng thổ nhưỡng và thảo mộc đặc hữu Việt Nam

Our list is organised by style and then by region, from the cooler aromatics of Alsace Riesling and Chablis Chardonnay to the structured grandeur of Left Bank Bordeaux and the sun-warmed opulence of Maipo Cabernet. Wines available by the glass (125 ml) are indicated alongside the bottle price.

# A Word on Pairing | Về việc kết hợp rượu & món ăn

Each dish on our à la carte carries a primary pairing (our house recommendation) and an alternative (for guests seeking a different register). Where the menu suggests, for example, Chablis, we propose Louis Latour « La Chanfleure » — a faithful, age-worthy expression. Where it suggests Pinot Noir de Bourgogne, we pour Louis Latour « Les Pierres Dorées » — Beaujolais-adjacent Pinot of clarity and aromatic lift. Our Sommelier remains at your table for tableside guidance.

# Service Notes | Lửu ý phục vụ

All prices are in (,000) VND and exclude 10 % VAT and 5 % service charge.

Champagne & white wines are served at 8–10 °C; Rosé at 10–12 °C; light reds at 14 °C; structured reds at 16–18 °C.

All bottles are decanted or polished at the table upon request.

A corkage fee of VND 500,000 per 750 ml bottle applies to wines brought from outside; limit 2 bottles per table.

Vintages may vary with availability. Our Sommelier will inform you of any substitution before service.
